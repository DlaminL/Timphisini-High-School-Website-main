Getting Admitted


At Timphisini we welcome everyone all around Eswatini, we are all about excellence though so kids with better results are of advantage when it comes to being admitted to the school.We accept applications at the start of the school semester, we accept applications from students who want to be admitted at Form 1, 2 and 4 every year.

Requirements

Requiremants for new students are

    A pass from previous school for form1 and the latest report for form2 and 4
    Guardian or parent's concern
    Birth certifcate copy
    passport picture
